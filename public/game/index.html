HOW TO PLAY

🎯Goal

Be the first commander to land a starship on the opposite edge of the galaxy and own more planets than the enemy.

🚀Your Forces

You command 2 red starships at the bottom. The alien controls 2 green ships at the top. Each turn, every ship can move once and fire its laser once.

🪐Planets

Land on a free planet to capture it. Land on an enemy planet to steal it. The more planets you control, the closer you are to victory.

🔫Lasers

Fire a laser to push back any enemy ship in a straight line — great for defending your planets or stealing enemy ones by force.

⚡Electric Walls

Place an Electric Wall on the grid to block enemy ships and lasers. You start with a limited supply — use them wisely!

🏆Victory

To win you must both:

  • Reach the alien's home row at the very top, and
  • Control more planets than the alien at that moment.
